Curriculum Vitae

About me

I obtained my PhD in Mathematics at Instituto Superior Técnico, University of Lisbon.

Research interests

My main area of research is ergodic theory and dynamical systems. In particular, I have been working in dimension theory, multifractal analysis and thermodynamic formalism. Recently, I've become interested in the intersection of dynamical systems and machine learning theory.
